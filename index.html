Ronk, Papes, Snips!

How to Play: Type the name of a master of rock, a master of paper, or a master of scissors and then the computer will also choose a master. Rock master, Ronk, beats the scissor master, scissor master, Snips, beats the paper master, and paper master, Papes, beats the rock master. Depending on who you choose, you either win, lose, or tie.
